Meet Jané

Lay Counsellor | BA Hons (Psychology)

The youth of South Africa is especially close to my heart. My five years of teaching experience, along with my role as a youth worker and school counsellor, have given me valuable insight into the challenges faced by learners, teachers, and parents. I believe it is part of my calling to walk alongside young people as they grow into resilient, confident, and purposeful individuals.

I work with not only the youth but anyone who needs a sounding board. I am highly organised and efficient, approaching each client with positivity, empathy, and perseverance to support meaningful growth and lasting outcomes.

I practise a person-centred approach, allowing clients to create their own path and discover their potential through the counselling process. With the right guidance, supportive structures, and faith as small as a mustard seed, I believe clients can climb even the greatest of mountains.
